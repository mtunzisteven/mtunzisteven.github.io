Site Purpose

This is a weather site that is designed to help people who live in Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven with an accurate and reliable weather focast. It helps all individuals in the area prepare adequately for the emediate future and be safe in times of storms. When people need to know what to wear, what to take on their hike, or to just check if it's the right day to hang laundry outside, this is the website that helps them make an informed dicision with confidence.
