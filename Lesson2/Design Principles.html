PARC: Contrast

Renewable Energy Solutions

Desctription

The greatest and best use of contrast I found, comes from the website of a solar energy solutions company. The colors of the logo are used throughout the website, but there is no conflicting, as they are coordinated to bring the best contrast. When the background is white, the text is black, and when the background is a dark color, the text is white.

PARC: Alignment

BYU-Idaho

Desctription

Each item in this website is a beautiful display of alignment. The navigation menu, in the form of a burger menu, is aligned to the page title, the "Campus VIdeos" call to action, and the two items on its left. The first and the second text of the top image is aligned to the call to action tilte on the bottom left. This website successfully applies this principle throughout the pages.
