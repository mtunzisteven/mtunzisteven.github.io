Bern Switzerland Temple

The Bern Swizterland Temple is located in the City of Bern. It was announced on the the 1st of July, 1952. Its ground breaking was done on August 5th, 1953, just over a year and a month since it was announced. 2 years after the ground breaking was done, on September 11, 1955, this temple was dedicated. Saints in this area have continued to enjoy the blessings of this temple for over 60 years. It is was rededicated in 1992. on October 23.
